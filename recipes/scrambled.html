Scrambled Egg Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for scrambled eggs. It's the best way to cook an egg.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • butter or oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • msg (NOT OPTIONAL)

Steps

  1. Start by heating up the pan. We should move fast like we're already late for work.
  2. Crack the eggs into a bowl (hit them on a flat surface to avoid shells in the eggs).
  3. Put salt, pepper and msg to taste.
  4. Put some butter on the pan, at least enough to cover the surface, more if you're an obese fuck.
  5. Beat the eggs with a fork or whisk them if you're a rich asshole.
  6. Pour the eggs into the pan.
  7. Start stirring, how fast depends on how big you want the curds to be.
  8. If it looks like it's almost but not yet cooked, take it off the heat. It's done.
